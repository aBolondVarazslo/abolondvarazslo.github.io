About

A trusted name in North Petherton & Bridgwater

Woodlands Hair Design is a friendly, personal hair salon serving Bridgwater and the surrounding area. With over 30 years of professional experience, you'll always be in confident, caring hands.

Whether you're coming in for a classic cut, a fresh colour, a perm, or a fashion transformation, every appointment is treated with the same attention to detail and dedication to making you feel great.

Children are always welcome. From first haircuts to school-ready styles, the relaxed, unhurried atmosphere means even young ones feel right at home.
